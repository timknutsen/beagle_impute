Research Focus

Current Projects

Our lab combines computational and experimental approaches to study genome evolution across a diverse range of organisms.

🧬
Genome 3D Structure & Whole Genome Duplication

Investigating how the three-dimensional organisation of chromatin changes after whole-genome duplication events and its functional consequences.

🍄
Fungal Genome & Trait Evolution

Exploring the evolutionary forces that shape fungal genomes and how genome-level changes translate to trait diversity.

🐟
Salmon Genetics, Physiology & Welfare

Using genomics to improve understanding of Atlantic salmon biology, health, and welfare in aquaculture settings.

🌿
Eukaryote Genome Diversity in Norway

Sequencing the remarkable diversity of eukaryote genomes present in Norwegian ecosystems to build a reference resource.
