Principal Investigator

Geir Kjetil Sandve

Professor & Group Leader

Geir Kjetil Sandve is a professor at the Section for Genome Biology, Faculty of Biosciences, Norwegian University of Life Sciences (NMBU). His research centres on computational genomics and the evolutionary and functional analysis of genomes.

He has made significant contributions to our understanding of transcription factor binding sites, regulatory evolution, and genome-scale patterns of variation.
